Side of the Road, Center of His Will

Posted August 2025

And then it hit me...."Was I meant to be here?" Not just “here” as in stranded on the side of the road with a flat tire and the smell of burnt rubber in the air, but here in this exact moment. The sun was beating down, I was on the way to the library, and all I could think was how inconvenient this was. But somewhere between the frustration and the silence of the empty road, I realized God had something to say. I had a glorious morning. I was blessed with an internship and as I am leaving for the library, a couple stops me about my tire being flat. I am immedistely frustrated. They help me fill up my tire and tell me a tire place to go to that could help me and the people are very nice. I BARELY made it to the shop...

“Nothing I do will ever cancel out God's will for my life.”

Recently, I had a burst of faith not only in myself, but in God. It's like God turned off all the anxiety in my body. I started seeing visions of me in living my dreams...

— Luxe
